E Sreedharan

The man who changed the face of public transport in India

Some of major public transport projects guided by Sreedharan

  • 1964 : Repaired the pamban bridge within 46 days and received Railway Minister's Award
  • 1970 : In charge of the implementation, planning and design of the Calcutta metro, the first ever metro in India
  • 1981 : Under Sreedharan's leadership, the cochin shipyard launched its first ship, the MV Rani Padmini
  • 1987 : Konkan Railway of 760Km was built within 7 years under his stewardship
  • 1997 : Successfully completed Delhi Metro Project within Budget and was called Metro Man by media

    • Presently he is the Principal Advisor and member of different metro projects in India like Lucknow Metro, Jaipur Metro , Kochi Metro and Coimbatore Metro.
